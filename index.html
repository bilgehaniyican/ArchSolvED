ArchSolvED

-ArchSolvED designed to help architects with spatial organisation of schools.

-It uses Ministry of Education's guidelines and regulations as measuring metrics.

-It gets involved in early design stage of architectural design process

-As the result application generates simple schemes with it's success rate regarding spatial orientation

-The architect picks the suitable scheme and continues with the design process


-This software is developed as a part of the PhD thesis at Karabuk University
